FAQs

What's included in your plan?

Simply Stellar covers everything required to build an entirely customised website. This includes: domain registration, web-hosting, wireframing, private photography, web-design, web-development and all neccessary updates.

No. You also reserve the right to cancel your service at any time at no cost.
(We're flexible like that!)


Do you require a deposit before starting a project?

We require your first month's payment as a deposit along with your signed project proposal to commence work.


Expertise and Technology

What is your process for testing websites before launch?

Our websites are thoroughly examined in our testing environment. We check that your website is responsive across devices and passes our development standards. At this stage, you will have the opportunity to interact with your website, provide input and request changes.


Do you offer website hosting and domain name registration services?

Domain registration and website hosting is included.


Maintenance and Support

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support for the websites you develop?

We offer unlimited minor edits to your website. This includes edits such as changing opening hours, replacing images or updating business contact details. Major changes, such as designing additional pages or developing and integrating new features, are not included and will incur a cost.


What is your policy on revisions and changes during the web development process?

We believe your input is critical. We provide you with the opportunity to openly share your feedback and suggest changes at major milestones during the creative and development process.


Accessibility

What is web accessibility and why is it important for my website?

Web accessibility is crucial because it enables people with varying abilities to freely access your website. Ignoring web accessibility standards restricts people with visual, auditory, motor and other impairments from accessing your website.


How do you ensure that websites are accessible to people with disabilities?

We adhere to WCAG 2.0 accessibility guidelines. Throughout the design phase, we place a strong emphasis on legibility of fonts, high contrast between elements and creating a uniform layout with a distinct header hierarchy. In the development phase, we craft purposeful alt-text for images and descriptive text for links, ensure keyboard accessibility and provide clear labels and instructions for form fields.

We endeavour to stay updated on emerging accessibility standards and make all necessary updates to ensure continued compliance.
